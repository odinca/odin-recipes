Spicy Tomato Pasta

Beautiful combination of tomatoes, chillies, basil and garlic. Perfect for any occasion.

Ingredients

  • 1 red chilli
  • 5 garlic cloves
  • 2tbsp tomato paste
  • 1tbsp oregano
  • 3 tins plum tomatoes
  • Handful of fresh basil

Steps

  1. Place pot on medium heat. Add a couple of tablespoons of oil, followed by the chilli.
  2. Add the tomato paste and oregano and give everything a stir.
  3. After a minute or so, add the tinned tomatoes.
  4. Bring to a gentle simmer then stir in the basil.
  5. Partially cover with lid and let simmer for 20-30 minutes.
  6. Take off the heat and wait a few minutes before blitzing with a stick blender.
